Crates System

Unlock a variety of rewards through our exciting crate system! Crates range from Common to Legendary, offering items and perks tailored for every type of player.

  • Common Crates: Perfect for starters with useful tools and rewards.
  • Rare Crates: A step up, providing exclusive items.
  • Epic Crates: High-value rewards for advanced players.
  • Legendary Crates: The ultimate crate with the best possible loot!

NPCs

Interact with friendly NPCs in the game! They provide:

  • Quests to enhance your gameplay experience.
  • Exclusive trades and items.
  • Guidance for new players to explore our world.

EclipseMC offers a unique and custom-tailored experience for every player:

  • Events and competitions with exciting prizes!

Community Features
